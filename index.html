Accident Prevention

The noise around the user is captured in real-time through his/her smartphone. Using Deeping Learning we recognize and classify the sound. As the deep learning model is available on the smartphone itself and can be used offline, this process takes place instantaneously. In case of emergency, example- speeding car, train honks etc, the user is notified through sequence of vibrations and visual notifications.

Communication using Sign Language

The Application detects and analyses the Indian Sign Language(ISL) gestures and convert them into the required regional Indian language in both text and audio formats. Apart from recognizing the alphabets in the ISL, the app will also recognize words and phrases used in everyday vocabulary. This can be achieved using Hand Gesture Detection and Pose Detection , since gestures for words in ISL involve the movement of the Palm as well as the Hand.
